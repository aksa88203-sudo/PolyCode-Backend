# Module 03 — Quantum Fundamentals

## What You'll Learn
- The mathematics of qubits (Dirac notation, Bloch sphere)
- Single-qubit gates and their effects
- Multi-qubit systems and tensor products
- Quantum measurement and probability
- Quantum circuits in Q#
