## Study Tips 💡

### Effective Learning

1. **Code Every Day**
   - Even 15 minutes helps
   - Consistency beats intensity

2. **Type Everything**
   - Don't copy-paste
   - Typing builds muscle memory

3. **Break Things**
   - Experiment freely
   - Mistakes teach you

4. **Build Real Stuff**
   - Automate YOUR tasks
   - Solve YOUR problems

5. **Review Regularly**
   - Revisit old code
   - Refactor and improve

### When Stuck

1. Read error messages carefully
2. Check [Troubleshooting](Troubleshooting.md)
3. Google the specific error
4. Ask in communities
5. Take a break and return fresh
